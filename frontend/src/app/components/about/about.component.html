About Magic Cloud

Magic Cloud is maintained and distributed by Server Gardens. In addition to delivering amazing Open Source frameworks, Cloud Systems, No-Code and Low-Code tools, we also provide services such as hosting, consulting, teaching, etc.

If you need hosting, and/or consulting services on top of Magic Cloud, you can contact us at thomas@servergardens.com, or visit our website for more information. We also provide a whole range of Micro Services on top of Magic Cloud, such as translations, enterprise email components, enterprise chat clients, etc. Either hosted by us on our servers, or hosted on premises by you. Visit the Bazar to see a list of components we provide.
